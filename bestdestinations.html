Tokyo

Come visit Tokyo! A thriving city in Japan with tons of culture. For around 900 euros you could visit this beautiful place and enjoy the diverse food and people. There are many shops to buy cool things and even more museums you can visit. There are also many beautiful gardens with green grass. The city also has an impressive, and sad history, with many parts having been destroyed in the last 100 years. But these events gave rise to the beauty of the new buildings in Tokyo.

New York City

New York City might be the most well known city of the world. And you can come visit it for the modest price of 700 euros! Enjoy the beautiful view of the skyline filled with skyscrapers and buildings. The city is also filled with the most varied culture of any city in the world. You can find any type of food, person, activity you'd like.

Beijing

Beijing, the capital of China, the country with the most inhabitants of the world. For around 800 euros you could visit this beautiful city. Visit the Forbidden City or walk along the majestic Great Wall of China. Enjoy the nice food and people. Maybe even visit the Olympic park or other parts of China near Beijing. There are tons of possibilities.

Sydney

Go to Sydney! Not the capital of Australia but maybe the most well known city of Australia. For around 1000 euros you can visit the Sydney Opera House! Maybe even enjoy an opera show yourself. Otherwise you could go to Bondi Beach and enjoy the sun while learning how to surf! Because of where Sydney lies on the world, the temperatures are nice throughout the whole year. So go whenever you want!

Capetown

Why not visit Capetown? The capital of South-Africa! You'd only have to pay around 800 euros to visit this beautiful place. Have a look at the Sanbi-Kirstonbosch gardens. Or maybe take a walk through Bouders Beach. The weather is also relatively nice throughout the year in Capetown so you can visit whenever you want!

Chicago

Or you could come to Chicago! An alternative choice to New York City for many people where you also get to enjoy the vividness of the United States. For the decent price of 800 euros you can experience this city for yourself. Come see the Art Institute of Chicago or try all the types of food you like! Chicago also has many fun activities, even if you're with family, alone or your partner.
